Privacy Policy

Z-Astro-Calculator — last updated March 2026

The app itself

Z-Astro-Calculator is a static single-page application. It runs entirely in your browser and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking of any kind. All calculations happen locally on your device.

Hosting

This website is hosted via GitHub Pages. When you visit this site, GitHub may automatically collect certain technical information including your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit, for security and operational purposes. This data is collected and managed by GitHub, Inc. under their own privacy policy.

For details on how GitHub handles this data, please refer to:
GitHub General Privacy Statement
GitHub Pages — Data Collection

Local storage

The app may use your browser's local storage to save preferences (such as language selection) on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Third-party links

The app contains a link to the GitHub releases page. Clicking it will redirect you to GitHub, where their privacy policy applies.
